What To Expect

We believe in full transparency and making this process as easy as possible, which is why we have a simple 7 step process.

1. Design

We'll help design and put your ideas on paper. You may already have a plan, if so, we can move forward to your specification sheet.

2. Specification Sheet

This is a description of your remodel in every detail. We meet with you to get all of your selections, helping you to pick out everything from flooring and baseboards to cabinets and countertops.

3. Proposal/Sign Contracts

When you accept the specification sheet and estimated cost, we'll sign a construction agreement.

4. Permits

This ensures your kitchen or bath is compliant with all building & energy codes. A permit is issued once all plan requirements are met.

5. Start Project

Once steps 1-4 are complete, we will schedule to begin work!

6. Inspections

Inspections are held at various stages of the remodeling process to ensure the project complies with all building codes

7. Project Completion

When final inspection is complete and signed off by the inspector, your final walk-through will be scheduled.
