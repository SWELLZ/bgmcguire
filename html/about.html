OUR COMPANY

B.G. McGuire Construction, Inc. opened it's doors and officially began home remodeling services in 2017, and company owner Brian McGuire, has been in the business for over 20 years.

Brian McGuire has a passion for utilizing his expertise and home construction experience to serve his Lodi, Manteca and Stockton clients, and takes full ownership and pride in every one of the company's home additions, renovations, and remodeling projects.
